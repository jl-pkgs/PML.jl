PMLV2 (Penman–Monteith–Leuning Version 2) Evapotranspiration model

Arguments

  • Tavg : degC
  • Rs : W m-2

PML.Param_PMLV2 — Type
struct Param_PMLV2{FT<:AbstractFloat} <: AbstractETParam{FT}

Fields

  • Alpha::AbstractFloat: initial slope of the light response curve to assimilation rate, (i.e., quantum efficiency; μmol CO2 [μmol PAR]⁻¹)` Default: 0.06

  • Thelta::AbstractFloat: initial slope of the CO2 response curve to assimilation rate, (i.e., carboxylation efficiency; μmol m⁻² s⁻¹ [μmol m⁻² s⁻¹]⁻¹) Default: 0.04

  • m::AbstractFloat: stomatal conductance coefficient Default: 10.0

  • Am_25::AbstractFloat: carbon saturated rate of photosynthesis at 25 °C, μmol m⁻² s⁻¹ Default: 50.0

  • VPDmin::AbstractFloat: parameter to constrain gc, kPa Default: 0.9

  • VPDmax::AbstractFloat: parameter to constrain gc, kPa Default: 4.0

  • D0::AbstractFloat: Default: 0.7

  • kQ::AbstractFloat: extinction coefficients for visible radiation Default: 0.45

  • kA::AbstractFloat: extinction coefficients for available energy Default: 0.7

  • S_sls::AbstractFloat: Specific leaf storage, van Dijk, A.I.J.M, 2001, Eq2 Default: 0.1

  • fER0::AbstractFloat: Canopy cover fraction related parameter Default: 0.1

  • hc::AbstractFloat: canopy height, [m] Default: 1.0
